# SKILL: Life Management

**Purpose:** Personal organization, scheduling, and life management  
**Domain:** Human 3.0 goals, constraint management, task organization  
**Type:** Meta-Container Skill

---

## Overview

The Life Management Skill centralizes personal organization tasks including scheduling, vacation planning, and goal management. This skill is crucial for achieving **Human 3.0** goals by managing personal constraints and aspirations stored in the TS (TELOS) file.

---

## Core Capabilities

1. **Scheduling & Planning**
   - Appointment scheduling
   - Calendar management
   - Time constraint optimization

2. **Goal Management**
   - Personal goal tracking
   - Constraint management
   - Progress monitoring

3. **Vacation Planning**
   - Travel itinerary creation
   - Resource booking
   - Activity planning

4. **Task Organization**
   - Task creation and prioritization
   - Deadline management
   - Workflow organization

---

## Workflow Integration

### Primary Workflows

- `workflows/schedule-showings.md` - Appointment scheduling
- `workflows/plan-vacation.md` - Vacation planning
- `workflows/create-task.md` - Task creation and management
- `workflows/goal-tracking.md` - Personal goal management

---

## Context Management

This skill ensures the agent loads the "exact right amount of context at the exact right time" by:

- Accessing user's TELOS (TS) file for personal context
- Loading only relevant constraints for current task
- Progressive disclosure of personal data

---

## Tools and Commands

- `schedule-showings` - Schedule appointments
- `create-task` - Create new tasks
- `plan-vacation` - Generate vacation plans
- `track-goals` - Monitor goal progress

---

## Human 3.0 Integration

The Life Management Skill supports the transition to Human 3.0 by:
- Managing complexity of modern life
- Enabling focus on purposeful work
- Supporting self-actualization goals
- Optimizing time and resource allocation

---

## Integration Points

- **Input:** User goals, constraints, preferences from TS file
- **Output:** Schedules, plans, task lists, organized workflows
- **Context:** TELOS file, personal constraints, calendar data
- **Tools:** Scheduling tools, calendar APIs, task management systems
