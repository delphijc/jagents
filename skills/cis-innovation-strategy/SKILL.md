# SKILL: Innovation Strategy

**Purpose:** Strategic differentiation and business model innovation
**Domain:** Business Strategy
**Type:** Creative Intelligence Suite (CIS) Module

---

## Overview

The Innovation Strategy skill provides frameworks for identifying unique market positions and disrupting existing business models.

---

## Core Capabilities

1. **Blue Ocean Strategy**

   - ERC Grid (Eliminate, Reduce, Raise, Create)
   - Strategy Canvas

2. **Disruption Analysis**

   - Low-end disruption assessment
   - New-market disruption assessment

3. **Business Model Generation**
   - Business Model Canvas
   - Value Proposition Canvas

---

## Integration Points

- **Input:** Business context, competitor data
- **Output:** Strategic canvases, innovation roadmaps
- **Used By:** [Innovation Oracle](../../agents/cis-innovation-oracle/AGENT.md), [Product Manager](../../agents/product-manager/AGENT.md)
