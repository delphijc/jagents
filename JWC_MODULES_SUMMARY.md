#### 1. Zero Trust Architecture
**File:** `rules/zero-trust-architecture/RULE.md`

**Core Principles:**
1. **Verify Explicitly:** Always authenticate and authorize
2. **Least Privilege Access:** JIT/JEA with risk-based policies
3. **Assume Breach:** Micro-segmentation, encryption, analytics

**Architecture Components:**
- **Control Plane:** PDP (Policy Decision Point), PEP (Enforcement), PAP (Administration)
- **Data Plane:** IdP, CDM, Compliance, Threat Intelligence, SIEM

**Maturity Levels:**
- Level 0: Traditional (perimeter-based)
- Level 1: Initial (MFA for remote)
- Level 2: Developing (MFA for all, device health)
- Level 3: Advanced (risk-based auth, micro-segmentation, JIT)
- Level 4: Optimal (continuous auth, dynamic segmentation, AI/ML)

**Technologies:**
- Identity: Azure AD, Okta, Ping
- Network: SDP (BeyondCorp, Zscaler), Micro-segmentation (VMware NSX)
- Endpoint: EDR/XDR (CrowdStrike, SentinelOne)
- Data: DLP, CASB, Encryption

**Implementation:** 3-phase roadmap (Foundation 0-6mo, Enhancement 7-12mo, Optimization 13-24mo)
