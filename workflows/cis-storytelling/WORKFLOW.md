# WORKFLOW: CIS Storytelling

**Type:** CIS Workflow
**Agents involved:** Master Storyteller (Sophia)

---

## Purpose

Craft compelling narratives to communicate vision and engage audiences.

## Steps

1. **Identify Audience:** Who are we telling the story to?
2. **Select Framework:** Hero's Journey, Story Circle, etc.
3. **Draft Narrative:** Write the story beats.
4. **Refine:** Polish for emotional impact.

## Trigger

- Command: `*tell-story`

## Outputs

- Narrative Script
- Pitch Deck Storyboard
- User Journey Story
