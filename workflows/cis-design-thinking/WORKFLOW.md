# WORKFLOW: CIS Design Thinking

**Type:** CIS Workflow
**Agents involved:** Design Thinking Coach (Maya)
**Phases:** 5 (Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test)

---

## Purpose

A human-centered approach to innovation that integrates the needs of people, the possibilities of technology, and the requirements for business success.

## Steps

1. **Empathize:** Understand the user (Personas, Empathy Maps).
2. **Define:** State the problem (Point of View).
3. **Ideate:** Generate solutions.
4. **Prototype:** Create tangible representations.
5. **Test:** Validate with users.

## Trigger

- Command: `*design-thinking`

## Outputs

- Personas
- Problem Statement
- Prototypes
- Test Results
