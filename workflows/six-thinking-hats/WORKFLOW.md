# WORKFLOW: Six Thinking Hats

**Purpose:** Structured perspective-taking for comprehensive ideation  
**Skill:** Brainstorming  
**Complexity:** Medium  
**Execution Time:** 20-40 minutes

---

## Overview

The Six Thinking Hats workflow guides users through six distinct thinking perspectives to explore ideas comprehensively and generate well-rounded insights.

---

## Prerequisites

- Problem statement or concept to explore
- Brainstorming Skill loaded
- Open-minded mindset

## Workflow Steps

### Step 1: Setup (Blue Hat)

**Action:** Establish the session framework  
**Duration:** 5 minutes

1. Define the problem or concept clearly
2. Set session objectives
3. Determine order of hats to use
4. Allocate time per hat

### Step 2: White Hat - Gather Facts

**Action:** Collect objective information  
**Duration:** 5-7 minutes

1. List known facts
2. Identify missing information
3. Document assumptions
4. Note data sources

**Output:** Factual foundation document

### Step 3: Red Hat - Express Feelings

**Action:** Capture emotional responses  
**Duration:** 3-5 minutes

1. Share gut reactions
2. Express intuitions
3. Note emotional concerns
4. Identify enthusiasm areas

**Output:** Emotional landscape map

### Step 4: Black Hat - Critical Analysis

**Action:** Identify risks and problems  
**Duration:** 5-7 minutes

1. List potential failures
2. Identify weaknesses
3. Note constraints
4. Document risks

**Output:** Risk and concern log

### Step 5: Yellow Hat - Positive Exploration

**Action:** Explore benefits and value  
**Duration:** 5-7 minutes

1. List benefits
2. Identify opportunities
3. Note success factors
4. Explore best-case scenarios

**Output:** Value proposition document

### Step 6: Green Hat - Creative Alternatives

**Action:** Generate new ideas and approaches  
**Duration:** 10-15 minutes

1. Brainstorm alternatives
2. Explore variations
3. Combine ideas creatively
4. Challenge assumptions

**Output:** Creative alternatives list

### Step 7: Blue Hat - Synthesis

**Action:** Organize insights and plan next steps  
**Duration:** 5-10 minutes

1. Review outputs from all hats
2. Synthesize key insights
3. Identify patterns
4. Plan action items

**Output:** Synthesized insight report and action plan

## Best Practices

- Focus on one hat at a time
- Avoid mixing perspectives
- Allow equal time for each hat
- Document thoroughly
- Stay disciplined on current perspective
