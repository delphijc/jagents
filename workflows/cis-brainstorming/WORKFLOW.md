# WORKFLOW: CIS Brainstorming

**Type:** CIS Workflow
**Agents involved:** Brainstorming Coach (Carson)

---

## Purpose

Generate a high volume of creative ideas using structured techniques.

## Techniques

- **Six Thinking Hats:** Exploring 6 distinct perspectives.
- **SCAMPER:** Substitution, Combination, Adaptation, etc.
- **Role Playing:** Acting out scenarios.

## Steps

1. **Define Topic:** Clarify the focus.
2. **Select Technique:** Choose the best method (e.g., Six Hats).
3. **Ideate:** Generate ideas without judgement.
4. **Cluster & Select:** Group similar ideas and pick the best.

## Trigger

- Command: `*brainstorm`

## Outputs

- Brainstorming Log
- Selected Concepts
