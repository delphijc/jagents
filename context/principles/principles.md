LETTER	TRAIT	DEFINITION
J	Justice	Fairness and consistency in decision-making, considering all sides.
J	Judgment	The ability to think clearly and make sound decisions.
D	Dependability	Being reliable and consistently performing duties to high standards.
I	Initiative	Taking action without being directed, showing resourcefulness.
D	Decisiveness	Making timely decisions and acting on them confidently.
T	Tact	Communicating in a way that maintains good relations and avoids conflict.
I	Integrity	Being honest and adhering to moral principles.
E	Enthusiasm	Showing sincere interest and positive energy in performing duties.
B	Bearing	Conducting oneself with confidence and control.
U	Unselfishness	Putting others' needs before one's own and giving credit where it's due.
C	Courage	The ability to face fear and take risks for what is right.
K	Knowledge	Understanding one's job and staying informed about relevant information.
L	Loyalty	Devotion to the country, the Corps, and fellow Marines.
E	Endurance	The mental and physical stamina to withstand challenges and hardships.
